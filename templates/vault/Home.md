# 🏠 Home

Welcome to your **brain-os** vault — a PARA-organized second brain wired into Claude Code.

## Map
- 🎯 [[Projects]] — active, goal-bound work (under `01 Projects/`)
- 🗂️ **Areas** (`02 Areas/`) — ongoing responsibilities & systems
- 📚 **Resources** (`03 Resources/`) — reference knowledge & knowledge bases
- 🗄️ **Archive** (`04 Archive/`) — finished/inactive items
- 📥 **Inbox** (`00 Inbox/`) — capture zone (auto session-captures land here)

## How it works
- **Session start:** Claude is told the primary store is this vault + your current projects, and asks which project new work belongs to.
- **Session end:** a raw session note is dropped in the Inbox.
- **Nightly:** captures are archived + indexed, and the semantic search index (qmd) is refreshed.
- **Ask anything:** content questions are answered by searching this vault semantically.
- **Present a project:** say *"I want to present <project>"* to open its pitch canvas.

See `02 Areas/Brain-System & Betrieb` (if present) for the full system map.
